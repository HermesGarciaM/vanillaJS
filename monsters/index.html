VanillaJS project - Monsters Game!

Build a game inspired by the movie Monsters, Inc. and loosely based on the old PC game Minesweeper.

How long it took me to finish this project from scratch: 3 Hrs.

Click on a door to find your monster friends. Avoid the sock!

Icons by Jamie Dickinson, Nicky Knicky, Alvaro Cabrera, Eliricon, April Yang, tk66, Alex WaZa, Husein Aziz, and Yazmin Alanis.
